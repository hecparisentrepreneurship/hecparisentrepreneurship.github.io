HEC Paris Entrepreneurship Workshop

Date: December 11-12, 2025

Location: HEC Paris, France

Organizers: Johan Hombert, Jessica Jeffers

 

The HEC Paris Entrepreneurship Workshop is a small-scale workshop that brings together researchers working in the field of entrepreneurship and innovation. It offers great opportunities for research discussions and interactions inside and outside the seminar room.

 

2025 Workshop Call For Paper

The workshop will take place on the HEC Paris campus on December 11-12, 2025. We welcome submissions on entrepreneurship, innovation, and their financing.

The workshop will start on Thursday, December 11 with lunch and end on Friday, December 12 in the afternoon. Dinner will be held at a gastronomic restaurant on Thursday evening.

Submission deadline is September 15, 2025. Please submit your paper to hec-entrepreneurship@hec.fr and name the PDF as LASTNAME_Firstname_Title.pdf. Decisions will be communicated by the end of October.
